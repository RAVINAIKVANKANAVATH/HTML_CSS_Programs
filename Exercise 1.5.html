Five Brands of Soft Drinks

Coca-Cola
Coca-Cola is a carbonated soft drink produced by The Coca-Cola Company. It is the most popular soft drink in the world.
Pepsi
Pepsi is a carbonated soft drink produced and manufactured by PepsiCo. It is the second most popular soft drink in the world.
Sprite
Sprite is a lemon-lime flavored soft drink produced and manufactured by The Coca-Cola Company.
Fanta
Fanta is a fruit-flavored carbonated soft drink created by The Coca-Cola Company.
Mountain Dew
Mountain Dew is a citrus-flavored carbonated soft drink produced and manufactured by PepsiCo.
